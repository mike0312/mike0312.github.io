# About
Michael Frank Elizondo is an American Army War Veteran boasting over 14 years of experience in Information Technology. Born and raised in South Texas, Michael resides with his family in San Antonio, Texas.

Early Life and Military Service:
From a young age, Michael was inspired by his father's military service and knew he wanted to follow in his footsteps. In 2004, he fulfilled his dream by joining the Army. Quickly finding his passion in the 12B Combat Engineer career field, Michael embarked on a journey of dedication and skill.
